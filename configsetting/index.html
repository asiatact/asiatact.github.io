Night Parking Setting allows Residents to apply for Night parking at Property. Residents can apple Night Parking from QeyPlus Mobile App. Administers can Enable or Disable this feature from the backend dashboard.

Navigate to Setting > Configuration

Enable Over-night Parking: Toggle On to Enable it or Toggle off to Disable it

OverNight Parking Limit (Per Month): Number of Overnight Parking allowed per Resident Unit / Month

Avail Parking Lot: Daily Night Parking Available Lots

Enable Face Recognition: Toggle On to Enable Facial Registration Feature for Visitors

Show Visitor Pass QRCode: Toggle On to Show QRCode in visitor Pass

Visitor QRCode Scan & Go: Enable Visitors to Use Scan & GO

New Resident Approval: Toggle On to Enable Approval for all new Residents

Resident Facial Data Approval: Toggle On to Enable New Facial Approval.
