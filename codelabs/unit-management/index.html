To take advantage of features like delivery or host notifications, QeyPlus needs to "know" how to contact your Host / Unit. You'll provide your Unit / Host contact information to QeyPlus by creating and maintaining your Unit directory.

The directory should contain a Resident record for each Unit. These records have the Resident's name, cell phone number (optional), Call Preferences etc..

Once a Unit record is created in your directory, that Resident will have certain permissions based on which products and account configurations you use:

  • Receive host notifications from QeyPlus Visitors: During sign-in, a visitor will be able to select the employee's name in the host field.
  • Receive delivery notifications from QeyPlus Deliveries: When an administrator logs a delivery, the Deliveries will match the recipient name with their name in the directory.
  • Access their QeyPlus mobile app: Residents can log in on web and mobile to manage visitors, invites, and/or deliveries.
  • Navigate to the "Units > Units" from the top menu.
  • Click "Create"
  • Enter Unit details into the relevant fields, and then click 'Add'.

You can also manage your directory by uploading a CSV of Unit records. A CSV is a type of file. It stands for "comma-separated values." CSV files store data in plain text, and can be created and edited either in spreadsheet software (like Google Sheets or Excel) or a text editor (like Sublime Text).

In a CSV, each record consists fields separated by commas. If you open a CSV in a spreadsheet software, fields are separated into columns instead of separated by commas. For the purposes of managing your Envoy directory, most administrators find it easier to edit a CSV in spreadsheet software.
  • Navigate to Unit > Unit from the top menu
  • Enter Unit Number on the Search box at the top right corner and press Enter
  • Click Unit Number and Press "Edit"
  • Change Relevant Information and Click Save
