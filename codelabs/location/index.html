When you sign up for Visitors, the first thing you'll do is create your first Entry Point. For most of our customers, a Entry Point represents a physical officeâ€”the place where their visitors sign in.

Your QeyPlus account can have one or multiple Entry Points, depending on your needs. If you only have one office, then one Entry Point is great, but the flexibility allows larger companies to create Entry Points for offices in different cities or for multiple buildings on a central campus.

Each Entry Point has its own visitor log and settings, and it's easy to switch between Entry Points if you have global permissions. You'd want to create a new Entry Point anytime you want to keep visitor data separate.
  • Goto Setting > Entry Point
  • Click Create
  • Enter Entry Point Name
  • Click Save
