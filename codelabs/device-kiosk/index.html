Device management allows you to connect any Android to any QeyPluslocation. The act of connecting an Android Device with your location is called pairing. When an Android Device is paired to a location, visitors that sign in on that iPad create entries for that location (i.e., they will appear on the location's dashboard and in visitor logs).

You can pair multiple Android Tablets to one location. With multiple iPads at one location, multiple visitors can sign in at the same time, and their entries are created for the same location simultaneously.
  • Navigate to Setting > Devices
  • Click Create
  • Enter Device Name
  • Select "QeyVMS" in Device Dropdown
  • Select "Entry Point" Where you place This Device
  • Click Save

The system will generate 8 digit Registration code

Download QeyVMS Android App from Play Store into your tablet

Open the App and Enter 8 Digit Registration Code
