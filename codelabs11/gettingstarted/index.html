Download the QeyVMS app

QeyVMS's app is located on the Android's Play Store, to install the QeyVMSapp, follow the below steps.

Locate the Android's Play Store icon and tap on this to open the PlayStore.

  • Tap on the search option and type in QeyVMS.
  • Tap on QVMS
  • Tap on the download icon and the app will begin to install.
  • Once Installed Open the App
  • Enter 8 Digit Code in red coloe showing on your screen

  • Tap on Visitor Purpose
  • Enter Required Data on the Screen
  • Click Submit
